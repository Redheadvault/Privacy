Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how our 3D printing management application ("the App") handles your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your device. All information you enter into the App, including:

  • 3D printing orders
  • Model files and details
  • Filament information
  • Printer settings
  • Cost calculations

...remains stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. Your data is subject to your device's backup settings (iCloud, iTunes, etc.) which you control through your device settings.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Using device passcode/biometric authentication
  • Keeping your iOS updated
  • Following Apple's security best practices

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
