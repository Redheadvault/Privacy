About V3D Manager

V3D Manager is a comprehensive 3D printing management application designed to help you organize and track your 3D printing business. Manage orders, inventory, models, and calculate costs all in one place.

Key Features

  • Order Management: Track customer orders and print jobs
  • Filament Inventory: Monitor your filament spools and materials
  • Model Library: Organize your 3D models and files
  • Cost Calculator: Calculate accurate printing costs including materials, time, and energy
  • Printer Management: Keep track of multiple printers and their settings
  • Customer Database: Maintain customer information and order history

Getting Started

When you first open V3D Manager:

  1. Set up your printers in the Printers section
  2. Add your filament spools to track inventory
  3. Create or import your 3D models
  4. Start adding customers and orders

Common Questions

How do I add a new order?

Navigate to the Orders tab and tap the "+" button. Fill in the customer details, select the model, and specify print settings.

How does the cost calculator work?

The cost calculator factors in filament cost, print time, energy consumption, and any additional costs you specify to give you an accurate total.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud backup enabled for the app, your data will be included in your iCloud backups.

Troubleshooting

App is not responding

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device.

Data not saving

Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device. Check Settings → General → iPhone Storage.

Calculator showing unexpected results

Double-check your printer energy settings and filament costs in the Settings menu.
